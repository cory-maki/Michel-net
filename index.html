About Dr. Alkhalil

Two Specialties, One Mission: Better Health for Michigan Families

Dr. Michel Alkhalil brings a rare combination of expertise to his patients in the Detroit metro area. After completing residency training at St. Joseph Mercy Oakland Hospital, he pursued not one but two fellowship programs — one in Pediatric and Adult Sleep Medicine through Drexel University and Hahnemann University Hospital, and a second in Allergy & Immunology at the University of South Florida, Tampa.

That dual training is the foundation of everything at the AAIRS Clinic and Troy Sleep Center, where Alkhalil serves as Medical Director. The AASM-accredited sleep clinic and allergy and pulmonology practice treats conditions that are often deeply interconnected — allergies that disrupt sleep, respiratory issues that compound fatigue — under one roof, with one coordinating physician.

Recognized multiple times on Hour Detroit's Top Docs list, Dr. Alkhalil has spent his career delivering specialized, evidence-based care to the communities of Oakland and Macomb Counties.
